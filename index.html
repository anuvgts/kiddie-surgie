About Kiddie Surgie

The Kiddie Surgie team, led by Dr Balamourougane P, Dr Vinayak S. Rengan, and Dr Aseesh Varma, provides expert paediatric and neonatal surgery in Chennai. We specialise in treating paediatric surgical problems including hernias, urological, gastrointestinal, thoracic, and oncological conditions in newborns and children.

Our team offers expertise in Minimally invasive Paediatric surgery including Laparoscopy and Thoracoscopy, offering safer procedures with smaller scars, less pain, and faster recovery. From life-saving neonatal operations to routine day-care surgeries, our approach blends advanced surgical skill with a child-friendly environment.

At Kiddie Surgie, every child receives safe, specialised, and compassionate care guided by years of expertise and a commitment to excellence in paediatric surgery.
